The honest edge · what's proven vs what's real

Merged and internally honest — not yet deployed

The bones are real and the discipline holds under probing. But a custody project owes itself a truthful edge, so here is exactly where the line sits.

  • Fixed: the synthesized screenshot is now sealed as synthesized_sample, never a fake real capture; the kernel-absent receipt degrades instead of crashing; CI now drives a real spoke, so the cross-repo claim is covered, not just proven off-CI.
  • Not deployed. The crypto backend is the pure-Python dilithium-py fallback — functional, not load-proven. Every surface is a simulation or a local fixture; no real Foundry tenant, camera, or vendor dashboard has crossed the system.
  • Packaged. pyproject.toml ships the axm entry point; axm_console.cli:main runs as pip would wire it (verified 2026-07-06 — the pip-install-to-PATH step itself was not exercised in that sandbox). Not published to PyPI — install from the repo.
  • Every contract edge reconciled. GhostBox's ConversationShardRef (axm-chat) and PhysicalEvidenceEvent (axm-embodied) were probed against real spoke output, corrected to reality, and landed with custody-first observers — live-proven against the probe artifacts 2026-07-06; the suite (108/108) pins the replicas.
  • Deliberately absent. No interpretation layer — no OCR, no vision models. Pixels stay opaque until a human-gated, tiered annotation layer earns the right to read them.

Standing rules · non-negotiable

  • Simulate, don't wait. Known surfaces are simulated at fidelity and labeled as such — no task ever stalls on live credentials.
  • No interpretation without a gate. No OCR, no vision models; pixels stay opaque until a human-gated, tiered annotation layer earns the right to read them.
  • Every artifact declares its tier. Nothing silently becomes platform truth, identity, or legal proof.
  • Custody is genesis's alone. Spokes reference the sh1_ id; they never mint, copy, or override it.
